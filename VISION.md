# Vision - Tabletop Chess (Gemini)

## Overview
`chess-tt-gemini` is a digital chess experience designed specifically for high-end tabletop form factors—large, horizontal touchscreen surfaces where players sit physically opposite each other. Unlike traditional online chess, this application focuses on the tactile and social experience of over-the-board play, enhanced by digital precision and features.

## Core Philosophy
1.  **Face-to-Face Social Interaction**: The UI is optimized for two people sitting at a table. Elements are oriented to face each player, and the board is the central focus.
2.  **Tactile-First Interface**: Interactions are designed for large touch surfaces, prioritizing clarity, responsiveness, and preventing accidental moves (fat-finger protection).
3.  **Zero-Configuration Play**: A user should be able to approach the table, start a game in seconds, and play without managing accounts or complex settings.
4.  **Digital Enhancements, Not Distractions**: Features like move validation, move history, and a built-in chess clock enhance the game without cluttering the physical space between players.

## Target Environment
-   Large-format touchscreen tables (e.g., 40"+ displays).
-   Progressive Web App (PWA) architecture for offline reliability and full-screen "app-like" experience on dedicated hardware.
-   Physical setting: Cafes, board game lounges, or home gaming tables.
